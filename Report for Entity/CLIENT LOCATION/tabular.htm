. CLIENT LOCATION STATUS IDENTITY The status of the Client Location. Existing represents a Draft location (just captured as a place holder but not usable anywhere else in RealSuite), Planned location (a hypothetical location which either exists physically but not yet Active/Occupied by the Client OR a location under construction but not yet Active/Occupied by the Client), Active location (a location both Active from a business occupant perspective and Occupied by the client), Inactive location (a location which may still be under lease/owned by the client but not Occupied or Active from a business occupant perspective, workorders and work can still be conducted as the Client still has liability for the location), Pending Closure location (a site under it's closure procedure for which workorders can not be leveraged but believe Leases, Utilities and Projects can still be issued), and Closed location (no longer usable business wise and which work can not be conducted against).

. LEASED RENTABLE AREA Represents the cumulative spaced by which the tenants/occupants can utilize for their business. It is assumed that Usable cannot exceed Rentable. o Defined by BOMA standard and certified by an architect. In case of a building owned by a client, the building itself should have architect certificate for its Rentable Area (Building & Floor Rentable Areas). If such certificate does not exist, it could be requested / acquired. The field to hold this information is important and must be present. The logic for owned buildings should be such that it could be entered directly and cannot be overwritten by leases (a client might have a 3rd party renting the space, while the rest of the building is still occupied by the client).
